Formatting Survey Questions

Underline

  • How thick is the underline?
  • How far below the base text does the underline appear?
  • Is the hulet neteb at the end of an underlined sequence also underlined?
  • Is the other punctuation at the end of an underlined sequence also underlined?

Hulet Neteb

  • Does hulet neteb appear before or after a number? (before, after, both)
  • Does hulet neteb appear before or after a parenthesis? (before, after, both)
  • Does hulet neteb appear before or after terminating punctuation like “?” or “!”?

(before, after, both)

  • Does hulet neteb appear before or after a Latin script word? (before, after, both)
  • Is hulet neteb used in dates?
  • When right justified, does any ink from split words go past the ink of the hulet neteb?
  • Do words in isolation end with hulet neteb?
  • Is hulet neteb used as a comma or word separator?
  • Is hulet neteb word-bounded, or centered?

Spacing

  • Is other punctuation treated identically as hulet neteb?
  • Does extra white space appear arat neteb? How much?
  • How many characters are there per line?
  • How many lines are there per page?
  • Do paragraphs use indentation? How much?
  • Is the first paragraph of a section indentented?
  • Are lists, tables, or other structured components indented? How much?
  • How much space appears between paragraphs?
  • Does extra space appear before or after a heading? How much?
  • What justification style is used?
  • What line breaking style is used?
  • If justified text, is there a minimum number of characters per line observed?

Page Numbers

  • What numeral system is used (Ethiopic, Latin, Both)
  • Which numbering system appears on the outside of the page?
  • Do the numbers appear at the top or bottom?
  • What is the first numbered page?
  • Is preface page numbering used? How so?

Other Punctuation Usage

  • Are any irregular punctuation in use?  3 or 7 dot marks, section mark?  Etc?
  • Are any Latin punctuation, or numerals, in use? Are they Ethiopicized?
  • If ellipsis appears, with how many dots?  Does hulet neteb appear before or after it?
  • Describe other use of sub- or superscript.
  • Can a paragraph end with a closing punctuation?
  • What parenthetic symbol(s) are used?
  • What abbreviation style is used?
  • What quotation mark symbol is used?
  • Is a duplication symbol used? (generally »)

Lists

  • In a bullet list, what bullet symbol is used?
  • What list item marker is used?
  • What list item separator is used, if any?
  • Does space appear after the marker?
  • How does a paragraph follow from a list?

Rubrication (if applicable)

  • Is punctuation rubricated following a rubricated word?

Ordinals

  • Is ኛ raised?

Numerals

  • Are numbers fixed height?
  • Do the lower bars start at or below the baseline?

Emphasis

  • Is there any use of bold text?
  • Is there any use of italics?
  • Is the font size larger for chapters or sections?  How much so?
  • Is there any other form of emphasis used?

Other Layout and Formatting Usage

  • Describe the header.
  • Describe the footer.
  • How many columns are used?
  • How are dates formed?
  • How is a bibliographic citation formed?
  • How is the publisher name formed?
  • Describe the table of contents, if present.
  • Describe the index if present.
  • Describe overall structure (component breakdown).
  • Describe table formatting. Cell centering? Vertical numeral alignment? Etc.
  • Table caption style?
  • Image caption style?
